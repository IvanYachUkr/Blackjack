Welcome to the Game of 21!

RULES:
The goal of the game is to achieve a hand value of 21 or as close to it as possible without going over. If your hand value exceeds 21, you "bust" and lose the round.

COMMANDS:
1. hit - Draw another card from the deck.
   Example: hit

2. stand - End your turn and stick with your current hand value.
   Example: stand

GAME STATE FORMAT:
The game state will be displayed as follows:

Player ID: [Your Unique Player ID]
Your Hand: [List of your cards] (Total Value: [Sum of your card values])
Dealer's Hand: [List of dealer's cards, with the first card hidden] (Total Value: [Sum of dealer's card values, with the first card's value excluded])
Current Turn: [Whose turn is it]
Game Status: [Ongoing, Player Won, Player Lost, or Draw]

EXAMPLE GAME STATE MESSAGE:
Player ID: 123e4567-e89b-12d3-a456-426614174000
Your Hand: 10 of Hearts, 6 of Spades (Total Value: 16)
Dealer's Hand: Hidden, 7 of Diamonds (Total Value: 7)
Current Turn: Player
Game Status: Ongoing

STATISTICS:
- Number of Rounds Played: [Total number of rounds played]
- Number of Rounds Won: [Total number of rounds won by the player]
- Number of Rounds Lost: [Total number of rounds lost by the player]
- Number of Draws: [Total number of draws]

Good luck, and may the odds be ever in your favor!
